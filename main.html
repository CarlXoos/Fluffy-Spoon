About the Program

Help the Environment

Every bottle you recycle helps keep our streets, waterways, and natural spaces clean and beautiful. We work directly with local recycling facilities to ensure that all collected bottles are properly processed and transformed into useful materials.

Get Free WiFi

Trade plastic bottles for internet time and enjoy fast, reliable WiFi for all your online activities. Whether you need to complete homework assignments, attend video calls for work, stay in touch with family and friends, or simply browse the web, our high-speed internet connection has you covered.

Build Community

Join your neighbors in working together toward a cleaner, more connected area where everyone benefits. This innovative program brings people together around a common goal while providing essential internet access to those who need it most.

How It Works

01

Collect Bottles

Gather clean plastic bottles from your home, workplace, or neighborhood. Any size bottle works perfectly as long as it's empty, rinsed, and dry.

02

Visit Us

Bring your collected bottles to our location in Prenza II during operating hours. Our friendly team will greet you, count your bottles carefully, and explain how much WiFi time you've earned.

03

Get Access Code

Receive your unique WiFi token and password instantly after your bottles are counted. Each bottle gives you three minutes of internet time.

04

Connect Online

Use your access code to connect to our secure WiFi network from anywhere within range. Enjoy fast, uninterrupted internet for the duration of your earned time.
